Frequently Asked Questions

What are your shipping options?

We offer standard shipping (3-5 business days), express shipping (1-2 business days), and same-day delivery for select locations in Lagos. Shipping costs vary based on location and delivery speed.

What is your return policy?

We accept returns within 14 days of purchase for most products. Items must be in original condition with all packaging and accessories. Some products may have specific return conditions.

Do you offer warranty on your products?

Yes, all our products come with a minimum 1-year warranty. Premium products may include extended warranty options. Warranty details are specified on each product page.

How can I track my order?

Once your order ships, you'll receive a tracking number via email. You can use this number on our website's order tracking page or contact our customer service team for assistance.
