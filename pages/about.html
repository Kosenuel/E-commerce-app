About Kosenuel

Delivering premium Home-wares since 2015

Our Story

Kosenuel was founded in 2015 with a simple yet powerful vision: to provide Nigerians with access to high-quality Home-wares that enhance their daily lives. What began as a small shop in Lagos has grown into one of Nigeria's most trusted Home-wares retailers.

Our journey started when our founder, Emmanuel Okose, recognized a gap in the market for premium Home-wares backed by exceptional customer service. Drawing from his background in electrical engineering and passion for technology, he established Kosenuel with a commitment to quality, reliability, and innovation.

Today, we continue to uphold these founding principles as we expand our reach across Nigeria and beyond. We carefully curate our product selection, partnering with leading global manufacturers to bring you the best in smartphones, laptops, audio equipment, and wearable technology.

Our Mission

At Kosenuel, our mission is to empower individuals and businesses with cutting-edge technology that enhances productivity, creativity, and connectivity. We believe that everyone deserves access to high-quality Home-wares that are reliable, innovative, and user-friendly.

Quality

We are committed to offering only the highest quality products that meet our rigorous standards for performance, durability, and design.

Trust

We build lasting relationships with our customers through transparency, honesty, and exceptional service at every touchpoint.

Innovation

We continuously seek out the latest technological advancements to ensure our customers have access to innovative solutions.

Community

We are dedicated to making a positive impact in the communities we serve through education, sustainability, and social responsibility.
